# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Engineering Manager

### Role Summary
Engineering Managers ensure delivery capability, team health, and alignment between engineering and product priorities.

### Responsibilities
- Resource planning and capacity allocation
- Remove team-level blockers and escalate when needed
- Support career development and performance management
- Coordinate cross-team engineering efforts and dependencies

### Interactions
- Works with Project Manager to balance scope vs. capacity
- Aligns with Product Manager on prioritization trade-offs
- Supports Tech Leads and Developers on delivery and technical debt decisions

---

## Tech Lead

### Role Summary
Tech Leads drive technical direction, architecture, and code-quality practices for a given team or area.

### Responsibilities
- Make/advise on architecture and design decisions
- Lead technical design reviews and ensure maintainability
- Coach developers and promote engineering best practices
- Coordinate complex implementation plans across teams

### Interactions
- Partners with Developers on implementation details
- Consults Product Manager on feasibility and trade-offs
- Coordinates with QA on test strategy and coverage

---

## UX Researcher / Designer

### Role Summary
UX Researchers and Designers ensure solutions meet user needs and usability standards.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and validate design hypotheses
- Produce prototypes, UX acceptance criteria, and design assets
- Ensure accessibility and usability considerations are included early

### Interactions
- Collaborates with Product Manager to shape requirements and acceptance criteria
- Hands off designs and specs to Developers and QA
- Works with Accessibility Specialists when required

---

## Data Analyst / Data Engineer

### Role Summary
Data roles ensure product decisions are backed by measurable outcomes and correct instrumentation.

### Responsibilities
- Define success metrics and instrumentation requirements
- Build dashboards, reports, and perform outcome analysis
- Validate data quality and support experiments/analysis

### Interactions
- Works with Product Manager to set measurable goals
- Provides PM, PMs and stakeholders with signals for prioritization and retrospectives
- Supports Developers and DevOps with instrumentation needs

---

## Release Engineer / DevOps

### Role Summary
Release Engineers maintain the deployment pipeline and ensure reliable releases and rollbacks.

### Responsibilities
- Maintain CI/CD pipelines and deployment automation
- Define rollback and recovery procedures; create runbooks
- Validate staging and production readiness; support post-deploy verifications

### Interactions
- Coordinates deployment windows with Project Manager
- Supports Developers during releases and post-deploy incidents
- Works with QA and Security for release validations

---

## Security / Compliance Owner

### Role Summary
Security owners mitigate security risks and ensure compliance requirements are met.

### Responsibilities
- Run threat models and security reviews
- Maintain compliance checklists and ensure security scanning is applied
- Advise on remediation and risk acceptance

### Interactions
- Engages during planning and design with Tech Leads and Developers
- Reports重大risks to PM and Product Lead and coordinates mitigations

---

## Support / Customer Success Lead

### Role Summary
Support and Customer Success leads represent customer-facing signals and help triage and escalate customer issues.

### Responsibilities
- Triage incoming customer issues and identify severity/impact
- Prioritize bugs from customer perspective and manage communication
- Own incident communication for affected customers

### Interactions
- Feeds customer impact and trends into Product Manager prioritization
- Collaborates with Developers and PM on fixes and workarounds
- Works with Documentation owners for customer-facing updates

---

## Accessibility Specialist

### Role Summary
Accessibility specialists ensure products meet accessibility standards and guide implementation/testing.

### Responsibilities
- Perform accessibility audits and define A11y acceptance criteria
- Provide implementation guidance and verification steps
- Support accessibility testing and remediation

### Interactions
- Works with Designers, Developers, and QA to ensure accessible deliverables
- Advises Product Managers on A11y implications for scope and timelines

---

## Business Analyst / Requirements Specialist

### Role Summary
Business Analysts clarify requirements and translate stakeholder needs into actionable work items.

### Responsibilities
- Refine and decompose user stories; clarify acceptance criteria
- Document domain rules and edge cases
- Assist in stakeholder alignment and traceability

### Interactions
- Bridges Stakeholders and Product/Project Managers
- Works with Developers to reduce ambiguity during implementation

---

## Localization / Internationalization Owner

### Role Summary
Localization owners ensure product content and behavior are ready for additional markets.

### Responsibilities
- Prepare content and strings for translation and locale-specific behavior
- Coordinate locale testing and cultural reviews
- Track translation readiness and release dependencies

### Interactions
- Coordinates with PM, Designers, QA, and Support for internationalized releases
- Works with Release Engineers to stage translation rollouts
